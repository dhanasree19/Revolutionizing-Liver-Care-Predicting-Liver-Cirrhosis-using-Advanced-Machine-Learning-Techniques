Revolutionizing Liver Care

Liver cirrhosis is a chronic and progressive liver disease marked by irreversible scarring of liver tissue. If untreated, it can lead to severe complications and liver failure. This project aims to develop a machine learning predictive model for early detection and prognosis of liver cirrhosis.

Liver Prediction

Utilize advanced machine learning algorithms to predict liver health and detect potential issues early, ensuring timely medical intervention and better health outcomes.
